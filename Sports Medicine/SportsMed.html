Sports Medicine

By Advanced Career Education Center at Hermitage

This course introduces students to topics such as human anatomy and physiology, nutrition, medical terminology, injuries and illnesses, and legal and ethical issues in sports medicine. Students learn and demonstrate emergency care, taping and wrapping of injuries, and apply other hands-on skills for treating injury.
