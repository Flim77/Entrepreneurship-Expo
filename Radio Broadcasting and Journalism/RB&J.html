Radio Broadcasting and Journalism

Advanced Career Education Center at Hermitage

Description

Students learn practices related to managing and operating a broadcasting station. Students examine the radio industry, news reporting, station frameworks and protocols, radio sales and budgeting, and broadcast engineering.
